Our Story


Our Welsh family business has traded in South Wales for over 20 years and has been instrumental in the resurrection of Welsh Kilts across Wales.

Having outgrown two smaller premises, in 2015 we moved to a purpose-built 15,000 sq ft premises with free customer parking! We only supply as many hires as we can handle in any given week, to ensure we maintain our service and quality levels which are highly regarded across the South Wales region.

Thank you for considering us for your function!
